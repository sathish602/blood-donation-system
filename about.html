OUR STORY

A Vision for a Healthier
Tomorrow

Founded in 2018, LifeStream emerged from a simple observation: the process of connecting blood donors with hospitals was fragmented and urgent.

We built a platform that simplifies this life-saving journey, ensuring that no patient has to wait for a critical transfusion.

Our integrated management system allows for real-time tracking of blood inventory, donor eligibility, and emergency request fulfillment, bringing clinical precision to a compassionate cause.
